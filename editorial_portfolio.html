About the Designer
I design the invisible systems that make players feel something real — and stay up wondering why.

I'm Pray Cristanto, a Game Designer based in Lampung, Indonesia, with five years inside a professional studio. My career started as a programmer intern — I built and published a complete mobile game in one month. Then I moved into community management, where I learned how players actually behave. Then into design, where both halves finally clicked.

"Most people think game design is about ideas. It's really about measurement. When a player says this felt unfair — there's a number somewhere that's wrong. I find that number."
Pray Cristanto · Core Design Philosophy

My work has reached 500K+ organic downloads across three commercial titles on PC, mobile, and Nintendo Switch. Google and Microsoft have selected me for accelerator programs — not based on pitch, but on what I've shipped. I was named Best Game Designer by Gameloft Indonesia in 2023.

Project 01 / 03
Visual NovelUnity · C#PC · iOS · Android

The Sun Shines Over Us

Award-winning narrative game on mental health and bullying. Designed the complete branching choice architecture — 47 nodes, 6 verified endings.

Role
Game Designer
Team
~8 people
Duration
18 months
Downloads
500K+
★
Best Narrative Game — IGDX 2022 · Finalist LevelUP KL 2021 · Best Team Gameloft Indonesia 2023
  • Designed 47-node branching system across 6 endings — all verified accessible through 30+ natural playthroughs
  • Identified path lock-in sending 80% of players to same 2 endings — rebalanced Act 1 weights to fix
  • Built community from zero, contributing to 500K+ organic downloads with zero paid spend
Intention
All 6 endings feel reachable through natural play — no guide, no dominant path.
Process
Mapped 47 choice nodes in Google Sheets with weighted values per ending path.
Challenge
80% of playtesters hit the same 2 endings. Early choices locked the path before midpoint.
Solution
Redistributed Act 1 weights — shifted tension to mid-game. All 6 endings normalized.
Outcome
Players reported "my choices actually mattered." 500K+ downloads, zero paid acquisition.

Project 02 / 03
SimulationUnity · MachinationPC · Nintendo Switch

Matchmaker Agency

Casual simulation shipped on PC and Nintendo Switch. Designed 100+ hand-crafted events and a 30+ trait compatibility matrix — balanced across 3 full test cycles.

Role
Lead Designer
Team
6 people
Events Built
100+
Traits Balanced
30+
★
Best Game Designer — Gameloft Indonesia 2023 · Available on Steam and Nintendo Switch
  • Identified "Adventurer" trait generating matches at 3× expected rate — rebuilt scoring formula and rebalanced 40+ combinations
  • Redesigned event triggers so players engaged with all 100+ events rather than skipping them
  • Documented all systems in Google Sheets for full team reference — zero tribal knowledge
Intention
Make wait periods engaging, not empty. No single trait dominates.
Process
100+ events using a content framework. Full 30+ trait matrix in Google Sheets.
Challenge
Players skipping events entirely + "Adventurer" matching at 3× the expected rate.
Solution
Tied early events to familiar characters. Separated scoring formula — 40+ combos rebalanced.
Outcome
Wait periods became a feature. No dominant strategies at ship. Launched PC + Switch.
