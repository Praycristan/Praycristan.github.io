About the Designer
I design the invisible systems that make players feel something real.

I'm Pray Cristanto, a Game Designer from Lampung, Indonesia, with five years inside a professional studio. Started as a programmer intern — shipped a mobile game in one month. Moved into community management, learned how players actually behave. Moved into design, where both halves finally clicked.

"Most people think game design is about ideas. It's really about measurement. When a player says this felt unfair — there's a number somewhere that's wrong. I find that number."
Pray Cristanto · Core Design Philosophy

My work reached 500K+ organic downloads across three commercial titles. Google and Microsoft selected me for accelerator programs based on what I've shipped. Named Best Game Designer by Gameloft Indonesia in 2023.
